# Image Optimization

Nextra enables `staticImage` by default. That means ordinary Markdown images are automatically served through Next.js `<Image>` with optimized output: no manual `width` or `height`, lazy loading, blur placeholders, and no layout shift.

![Optimized diagram](./_assets/diagram.png)

You can also use `next/image` directly when you want full control over props:
